About Scouting Legion

Scouting Legion is dedicated to delivering reliable and engaging stories about the environment, climate change, and sustainability. We are passionate about making a positive impact through the power of information and awareness.

Our mission is to help people understand the importance of taking action against climate change and to inspire individuals to live more sustainably. At Scouting Legion, we believe that everyone has a role to play in protecting the planet for future generations.

Founded in 2024, we are a team of environmental enthusiasts, researchers, and writers who are committed to providing the most up-to-date information on climate issues. Join us on this journey to make a difference, one story at a time.
